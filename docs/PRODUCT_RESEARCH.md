# Product and Competitor Research

## Problem statement

RideNShine is a real motorcycle repair, spare-parts and bike-wash business in Fatullah, Narayanganj. Customers normally visit, call or message the Facebook page to ask about parts and servicing. Inventory is maintained in Excel and customer information is mainly kept through invoices. This creates repeated availability calls, no structured booking workflow, and no public source of current services, offers and verified feedback.

## Target users

### Primary
Motorcycle owners near Fatullah and Narayanganj who want to know whether a part is available and request a service date before travelling to the shop.

### Secondary
The RideNShine owner who needs one place to maintain products, stock status, services, offers, incoming bookings and public reviews.

## Competing and alternative solutions

Research checked on 16 June 2026.

| Solution | Strengths | Gaps for the RideNShine use case | Lesson used |
|---|---|---|---|
| BikePartsBD | Dedicated online catalogue for motorcycle parts and accessories, product search and online ordering. | Mainly an online parts-store experience; it does not represent RideNShine's local stock, service serial workflow and workshop fitting process. | Customers expect searchable categories, visible products and clear support contact. |
| Daraz Bangladesh motorcycle parts | Large marketplace selection, discounts, checkout, home delivery and reviews. | General marketplace; compatibility, trusted local fitting, workshop servicing and direct owner-controlled local stock are not its main purpose. | Product cards, availability and reviews help purchase decisions, but RideNShine should avoid copying unnecessary marketplace complexity. |
| MotoMechs Bangladesh | Professional motorcycle maintenance and repair information with a visible booking action. | A service-focused competitor; it does not solve RideNShine's exact inventory, local offers, review moderation and owner dashboard needs. | Online booking should clearly describe the service and lead to a simple request flow. |
| Yamaha/Suzuki online service booking | Structured service request for customers and reduced physical waiting. | Brand-specific ecosystem; RideNShine serves a local multi-brand customer base and also sells parts and provides bike wash. | Booking needs bike information, location/date details and later confirmation. |

## Differentiator

RideNShine combines five related needs for one real local business:

1. Owner-maintained product and stock information.
2. Service catalogue and no-payment booking requests.
3. Customer booking reference and status tracking.
4. Moderated reviews with optional service images.
5. Operations dashboard, offers, monitoring and error tracking.

The system deliberately does not force online payment. Customers commonly need the workshop to verify compatibility and assemble the purchased part. Home delivery can remain a phone-confirmed exception through local courier services.

## Product success indicators

- Fewer phone calls that only ask whether a product is in stock.
- Number of valid online booking requests.
- Percentage of bookings moved from REQUESTED to COMPLETED.
- Number and average rating of approved reviews.
- Time required for the owner to update stock.
- Feedback from owner and customers on ease of use.
